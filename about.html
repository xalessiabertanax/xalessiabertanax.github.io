A bit about me.

Hello, I'm Alessia.

I believe that meaningful products are best created at the intersection of deep empathy, strategic thinking, and ofcourse the technical reality to make it all reality.

The Person Behind the Process.

I’m a researcher fueled by empathy and a service designer fascinated by systems, constantly looking for ways to humanize complex challenges.

With a Double Master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction and Computer Science, I consciously operate at the intersection of human perception and technical reality. I look at digital products through a human lens—understanding how users process information, feel, and behave—while maintaining a sharp eye on what is technically feasible to build. Throw me into a complex ecosystem? I’ll map it. Need to align stakeholders around a shared vision? I’m on it. I make sure we don't just build things, but that we build the right things.

Research & Strategy Philosophy.

I believe true impact lives at the intersection of qualitative empathy and quantitative data. Every touchpoint in a user's journey should serve a purpose, every process should feel natural, and every strategic decision must be backed by real, validated user insight.


The Energy.

I thrive on connection and collaboration. Breaking down systemic complexity is my specialty, but my real passion is bringing diverse teams together so that every perspective is heard.

Whether I'm translating a technical constraint from engineering or pitching a service blueprint to stakeholders, I bring clarity and enthusiasm. I believe solving complex social problems requires alignment, accessibility, and positive energy, and I bring that to every team I join.
