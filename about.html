The Person Behind the Portfolio

Hi, I'm Alessia! I bring the energy. I’m a designer who cares deeply about people, but I don't stop at the design part.

I don't fit neatly into one box, and I don't want to. I am flexible, quick to adapt, and I love the process of figuring things out. Throw me into a project with new tech? I’ll learn it. Need to bridge the gap between creative vision and technical reality? I’m on it. I ensure that what we design is actually what gets built.

Cultivating Empathy

I believe that to design for people, you must understand them. My passion for travel isn't just about sightseeing; it's about exploring new cultures to truly empathize with people and understand their values and principles.

Just as we tailor an application or website to a specific target audience, traveling helps me understand the "user" in the first place. Immersing myself in these differences enriches the empathy that fuels my creativity.
