Case Study

Baker Hughes, Training Dashboard

Automating training analytics with an evidence-based Power BI dashboard using Design Science Research

Role: Services Intern
Year: 2024–2025
Duration: 12 months
Power BIPower AutomateStoryline 360Design Science Research

01

The Challenge

The team relied on manual tracking and standard NPS scores for training evaluation, offering no granular insight into why a training succeeded or failed.

Key Pain Points

1Training evaluations were manual, time-consuming, and lacked depth
2No centralized system to compare metrics across sessions
3Standard NPS scores provided no actionable detail
4Stakeholders couldn't make evidence-based curriculum improvements

02

The Process

01

Design Science Research

Used a Design Science Research approach to identify and integrate eight complementary metrics based on Kirkpatrick's framework.

02

Dashboard Architecture

Designed the information architecture and visual layout of the Power BI dashboard to surface actionable insights at a glance.

03

Usability Testing

Validated the dashboard through task-based usability testing with stakeholders, achieving 92% task completion and 87.8 SUS score.

04

Automation & Delivery

Implemented Power Automate workflows to reduce manual data processing. Built complementary e-learning modules with Storyline 360.

03

The Solution

An automated Power BI training evaluation dashboard integrating eight metrics beyond standard NPS, enabling stakeholders to understand not just if a training succeeded, but exactly why.

✓8 evaluation metrics based on Kirkpatrick's framework
✓Automated data pipeline via Power Automate
✓92% task completion rate in usability testing
✓87.8 System Usability Scale (SUS) score
✓E-learning modules built with Storyline 360

04

The Impact

The automated dashboard reduced data processing time and empowered stakeholders to make evidence-based improvements to training curriculum.
