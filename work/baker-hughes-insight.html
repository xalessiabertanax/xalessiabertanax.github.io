Case Study

Baker Hughes, The Insight Tool

Designing an MVP to capture customer pain points from the field, driven by user research and psychological safety

Role: Services Intern
Year: 2024–2025
Duration: 12 months
FigmaPower AppsUser InterviewsSurveysMVP Design

01

The Challenge

The team wanted to evolve their existing submission platform into something more accessible and engaging, ensuring that no valuable idea from the field went unheard.

Key Pain Points

1Customer insights from the field went unheard due to high participation barriers
2Public voting mechanisms discouraged candid, honest feedback
3Existing platform was not engaging enough for regular use
4No streamlined way to log a pain point quickly

02

The Process

01

Discovery & User Research

Partnered with manager to launch interviews and surveys. Discovered that the key to participation wasn't gamification, it was psychological safety.

02

Synthesis & Ideation

Analyzed survey results, extracting key takeaways and mapping them to features. The insight: prioritize speed and anonymity over public engagement.

03

MVP Design in Figma

Designed the MVP with a streamlined flow where users could log a pain point in under 30 seconds. Focused on lowering every barrier to entry.

04

Collaboration & Handoff

Delivered design assets while collaborating closely with manager to implement the logic in Power Apps. Set the stage for validation and iteration.

03

The Solution

A lightweight MVP strategically designed to lower barriers to entry, replacing public voting with a private, streamlined channel for capturing insights.

✓Log a pain point in under 30 seconds
✓Private, anonymous submission replacing public voting
✓Streamlined flow reducing friction at every step
✓Designed for psychological safety to encourage candid feedback
✓Figma prototype with full interaction specs

04

The Impact

The MVP was delivered as a foundation for implementation, designed to significantly lower the threshold for participation and encourage more candid feedback from the field.
