Projects

Brain Computer Interface

We developed an adaptive version of PacMan that adjusts its game mechanics based on the balance of cognitive workload and engagement of the participant. We worked with a Unicorn headset to measure the EEG data.

Virtual Reality Exposure Therapy for Train Staff

Safety in trains has been a highly public issue at NS and other train providers. The solution we created is a controlled virtual train environment where NS personnel can be gradually and safely guided back into the train again. Exposure therapy is a proven cognitive behavior therapy method which helps patients face their fears in a controlled environment.

Explaining Vehicle to Grid (V2G) through Animation

I conducted my bachelor thesis assignment about explaining the concept of Vehicle-to-Grid, which refers to using electric cars as a buffer for the electricity grid in the Netherlands. The animation elements were created in Adobe Illustrator and the Animation was made in Adobe After Effects.
