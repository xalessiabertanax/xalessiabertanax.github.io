How I Design .

A flexible process I can adapt to each product team.

1. Frame & Align

I start by clarifying the problem with stakeholders, understanding user journeys and constraints so we solve the right thing.

2. Explore with Users

Research (interviews, surveys, desk research) surfaces motivations, hurdles and success metrics that inform the experience.

3. Prototype & Iterate

I move from low-fidelity prototypes into high‑fidelity prototypes, validating early with users to reduce risk before building.

4. Ship & Learn

Paired with engineers, I refine the product by using analytics and feedback to inform the next iteration.
